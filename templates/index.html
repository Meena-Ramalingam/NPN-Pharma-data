Drugs

M01AB - Anti-inflammatory Drugs

Used to reduce inflammation and alleviate pain associated with conditions like arthritis.

M01AE - Analgesic Drugs

Relieves pain without affecting consciousness; used for mild to moderate pain.

N02BA - Pain Relievers

Manages pain from various conditions, including chronic pain and pain from injuries.

N02BE - Specific Pain Relievers

Targeted for specific types of pain, such as neuropathic pain or pain associated with cancer.

N05B - Psycholeptic Drugs

Treats psychiatric conditions such as anxiety, depression, and insomnia.

N05C - Sedatives and Related Drugs

Induces calmness and sleep, often prescribed for sleep disorders or severe anxiety.

R03 - Respiratory System Drugs

Treats respiratory conditions like asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD).

R06 - Antihistamines

Relieves allergy symptoms such as sneezing, itching, and runny nose.
